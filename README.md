A quick machine learning adventure in a field that interests me (finance) with PyTorch.
Used the following video as a quide and filled in the blanks with a bit of trial and error and external sources:
https://youtu.be/r1bquDz5GGA?si=OcDPiD757xKDT8mT

In practice the model is trained on 5 years (2015-01-01 to 2020-01-01) of Nvidia price data and a 10 day moving average.
The model is then evaluated on 5 years (2020-01-01 to 2025-01-01) price data.

As the model uses a 10 day moving average the result and accuracy of the model likely doesn't mean anything.

Mostly done to get familiar with making a model by hand and not just watching tutorial (have watched 1)
